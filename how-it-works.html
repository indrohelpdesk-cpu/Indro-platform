The Indro Process

We eliminated the friction of traditional freelancing. Here is the exact step-by-step process of how we protect both clients and developers through our fixed-price escrow system.

Step 1: The Blueprint

Post a Detailed Tender

Clients define the exact scope, timeline, and a non-negotiable fixed budget for their app. Clear boundaries prevent scope creep and endless revisions down the line.

1
Step 2: Financial Security

Fund the Escrow

Before any work begins, the client deposits the full budget into a secure escrow account. Developers know the money is guaranteed, and clients know their funds are safe until delivery.

2
Step 3: Instant Match

Developer Claims the Work

Zero bidding wars. A verified developer who meets the exact technical requirements accepts the fixed price and claims the tender. Development begins immediately.

3
Step 4: Quality Assurance

Delivery & 30-Day Warranty

The developer delivers the compiled app and source code. The client reviews the work against the initial tender. Once approved, the funds unlock. Every project includes a mandatory 30-day bug-fix warranty.
